About Us

Welcome to A&M Closet! We are dedicated to providing the best services to our customers. Our team is passionate about delivering high-quality products and ensuring customer satisfaction.

Started along the city of Balagtas, Bulacan in 2015, owners Matthew de Vera and Allaine Francisco started selling their products in a tiangge (market/flea market) with only just T-shirts as their main product. Eventually, they had a decision to make or have their own brand for their products as their family business. They now have a growing 18 A&M Closet branches nationwide, and their Dasmarinas Branch as our target subject. .
